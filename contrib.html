+-------------------------------+
       | Do you want to contribute to  |
       | PHOENIX? We're looking for... |
       +-------------------------------+

Engine Programmer: You will be responsible for implementing new features and reconstruct old functions (experience with C++ required), see our docs.

3D Modeler: We look for a 3D artist to model simple low poly ambient objects and armours according to original design concepts and given instructions and to fix some uv-mapping.

Animator: You'll be responsible to create and implement various human animations and specific object interactions, including work on the combat system.

Comic Artist: Work on spin-offs to our main project.

Graphic Artists: We need you to create low-res textures in the style of GOTHIC (primarily armours) as well as level textures for Act 2 (city); usually not more than 256x256 or at most 512x512 pixels.

Concept Artists: We look for concept artists to do some sketches for Act 2 of PHOENIX. This way you can take part in the design of the gloomy city of Khorinis in decay and help us to design dungeons and armors.

If you have questions or ideas, that are not already answered in the ARCHIVE README or the PHOENIX FAQ? You want to contribute something to the Gothic Archive? Or you think that you could help us in any other way or give us any kind of support? Then feel free to reach out to us.

You can contact us via Signal or join our Discord server.
